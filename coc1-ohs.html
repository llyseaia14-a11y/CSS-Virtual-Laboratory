OHS – Occupational Health & Safety

Overview

Occupational Health and Safety (OHS) refers to the practices and procedures implemented to ensure safety in the workplace. In Computer Systems Servicing, OHS is essential to prevent electrical hazards, injuries, and equipment damage.

Key OHS Rules

  • Wear proper PPE — including anti-static wrist straps.
  • Ensure the computer is unplugged before opening the case.
  • Keep liquids away from electronic components.
  • Maintain a clean and organized workspace.
  • Avoid touching circuitry without proper grounding.

Importance of OHS

Following OHS prevents accidents, protects expensive hardware, and ensures safe and efficient performance during maintenance.
